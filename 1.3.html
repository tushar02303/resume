About Me

Versatile Actor from Mumbai

I am a passionate and dedicated actor based in Mumbai, with over 8 years of experience in film, television, and theater. My journey in the entertainment industry began with theater in Pune, and I've since worked on various projects that have shaped my craft.

My acting style is characterized by emotional depth, versatility, and commitment to character authenticity. I believe in thoroughly researching each role to bring truthfulness to the performance.

I have trained under renowned acting coaches in Mumbai and have a strong foundation in both Indian and international acting techniques. My fluency in Hindi, English, and Marathi allows me to take on diverse roles across different mediums.

When I'm not on set, I enjoy watching world cinema, practicing yoga, and mentoring young actors at local drama schools.
