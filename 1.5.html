About Me

Mumbai-based Actor with Diverse Experience

I am a dedicated actor with a passion for storytelling and character development. My journey in acting began with theater in Pune, where I performed in numerous productions before transitioning to film and television.

I believe in the power of authenticity in performance and approach each role with deep research and emotional preparation. My training includes intensive workshops with renowned acting coaches in Mumbai and London.

My multilingual abilities (Hindi, English, Marathi, Gujarati) allow me to connect with diverse audiences and take on roles across different cultural contexts.
