About Me

Mumbai-based Actor & Director

Born and raised in Mumbai, I discovered my passion for acting at a young age through school theatre productions. I trained at the prestigious Film and Television Institute of India (FTII) and have since worked across multiple mediums.

My approach to acting is rooted in emotional authenticity and thorough character research. I believe in immersing myself completely in every role, whether it's for a major film production, a television series, or a commercial.

Fluent in Hindi, English, and Marathi, I bring cultural depth to my performances and have worked with some of the most respected directors in the Indian entertainment industry.
